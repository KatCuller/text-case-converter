Writing for the Web: Why Consistency in Text Case Is Critical

Consistency in your text case isn’t just about looking polished — it’s about building trust and improving user experience. When writing for websites, emails, or apps, using the same formatting throughout makes your message more readable and easier to follow.

Why It Matters

  • Professionalism: Mismatched case styles can make a site look amateur.
  • Readability: Clear structure improves scanning and comprehension.
  • Trust: Consistent design signals attention to detail and care.

Where to Apply It

  • Website headers and subheaders
  • Buttons and CTAs
  • Blog titles
  • Email subjects and greetings
