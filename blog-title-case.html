What Is Title Case and When Should You Use It?

Title Case is a style of capitalization where the first letter of most major words is capitalized. It's often used for headlines, titles, and headings to make content look polished and professional.

Examples of Title Case:

  • The Quick Brown Fox Jumps Over the Lazy Dog
  • How to Improve Your Writing With Consistent Formatting

When Should You Use Title Case?

Title Case is typically used in:

  • Blog post titles
  • Article headlines
  • Book and movie titles
  • Navigation menus

Using Title Case properly can improve readability and make your content appear more professional. Many people apply it inconsistently, which can hurt your brand’s credibility — especially in digital writing where first impressions matter.
